A bit about me

From a very young age I've been fascinated with all things web and technology. I've also had a life-long interest in creative and artistic endeavors, and I take great delight in learning and honing new skills toward manifesting those creative expressions. In my time after High School I worked many service related jobs where I acquired a strong customer service acumen.

Before long the itch to dive into the tech-field grew too strong to ignore any longer and I partook in the Emerging Digital Academy program where I was able to learn the essential coding and project management skills to take my first steps into working in the field which has interested me so long. During my time there I was exposed to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, React, Redux, Node, Postgres, C#, Git Workflows, Heroku, and many others.

I hope to continue this journey of discovery and learning of these fascinating technologies.
